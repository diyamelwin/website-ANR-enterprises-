About us

At ANR Enterprisers, we pride ourselves on being a trusted name in the production of high-quality buttons and bits. From intricate design to durable manufacturing, we deliver solutions that cater to a wide array of industries, including fashion, construction, and crafts. With years of expertise and a commitment to innovation, we blend traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology to meet the needs of our global clientele. Our mission is to provide products that not only enhance functionality but also add a touch of elegance and reliability to every project.

ANR, which is a proven name in rock drilling tools in India, is a leading global provider of rock drilling tools for construction, mining and water well segments.
